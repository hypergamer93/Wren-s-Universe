Name: Cisi
Star system: Iri
Planet type: Dwarf Planet 
Radius: 800 km
Description: The planet is small, but has a dense solid iron core. It's surface appears green from space, yet it has no vegetation. It's soil is rich in copper, which gives the planet its appearance. Its outer crust contains trace amounts of Cuprenum, any larger vains of this rare material are sparse. There are also fossilized remains of massive alien machinery dated to be ~65,000 years old. It's intended purpose, way of functioning and creators so far remain unknown.
