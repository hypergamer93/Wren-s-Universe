Name: Ilimi
Star system: Iri
Planet type: Dwarf Planet 
Radius: 1,200 km
Description: Ilimi is a small astronomical body located in the outer parts of the system, away from the star, made out of roughly 40% gold, 20% platinum and 3% iridium. Presence of those elements is visible from space, as they form large patches of pure ore around the planet's eauator. 

As of 62, 374 both species in the system have yet to sent large mining operations to the planet, but it has already been mapped by unmanned satelites.
